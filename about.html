Our Story

Crafted with intention.
Made with love.

Two brothers, one vision: to bring India's ancient skincare wisdom to your daily ritual. Every product tells a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and pure natural ingredients.

The Beginning

From our grandmother's kitchen to your bathroom shelf

Bighi Brothers began in 2022 in a small village in Kerala, where our grandmother would prepare natural skincare remedies using ingredients from our family farm. What started as a way to share her wisdom with family and friends soon became a mission to bring authentic, handcrafted skincare to conscious consumers everywhere.

We believe skincare should be simple. No synthetic chemicals, no unpronounceable ingredients—just pure, natural goodness that your skin recognizes and loves.
