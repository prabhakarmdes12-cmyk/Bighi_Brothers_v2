Lasts ~30 days with daily use. We'll remind you before you run out.

Each bar of Marigold Mountain Soap is cold-processed in small batches at our workshop. We use the traditional saponification method, which preserves the glycerin in the soap — your skin's natural moisturizer.

Marigold (Calendula) is renowned for its soothing properties. Combined with organic turmeric's anti-inflammatory benefits and cedar's grounding aroma, this soap is suitable for all skin types, especially sensitive or reactive skin.

Benefits

  • Gentle daily cleansing without stripping natural oils
  • Soothes irritation and redness
  • Anti-inflammatory properties from turmeric
  • Natural antibacterial protection
  • Leaves skin soft, never tight

Morning Ritual: Lather between hands or on a washcloth. Apply to damp face and neck in gentle circular motions. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. Follow with toner and moisturizer.

Evening Ritual: Use as a second cleanse if wearing makeup or sunscreen. Can be used on body as well — particularly beneficial on arms and shoulders.

Pro Tip: After rinsing, while skin is still damp, apply our Shea Moisture Cream to lock in moisture before it evaporates.
