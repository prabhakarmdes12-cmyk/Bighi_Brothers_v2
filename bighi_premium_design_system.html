How to Use

The Marigold Mountain Soap is our original formula — the product that started everything. It was built for Indian skin, which faces hard water, humidity, and UV stress that most Western skincare formulas simply don't account for.


Turmeric brightens over time. Neem clears acne bacteria without the harshness of chemical cleansers. Calendula soothes inflammation after the battle. Together, they make the most effective morning cleanser we've ever made.

Saponified Coconut Oil · Deep-cleansing, lathers richly


Turmeric (Curcuma longa) · Antioxidant, brightens, reduces hyperpigmentation


Neem Leaf Extract · Antibacterial, acne-clearing, anti-fungal


Calendula Flowers · Anti-inflammatory, cell regeneration, calming


Pure Essential Oils · Natural fragrance, aromatherapy effect

Step 1: Wet your face with lukewarm water.


Step 2: Lather the soap in your palms for 10 seconds before applying to face. This activates the botanicals.


Step 3: Massage for 60 seconds in circular motions. Take your time — this is the ritual.


Step 4: Rinse with cool water. Pat dry. Never rub.


Pro tip: Keep your soap on a dry soap dish between uses. The glycerin layer that forms is valuable — don't wash it away.
